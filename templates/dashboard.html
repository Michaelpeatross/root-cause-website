📱 Connect Health Devices & Wearables

Upload data from your Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, Whoop, or other health monitors. Grok will analyze heart rate, sleep, steps, HRV, activity and more, and include insights in your reports.

Easiest way to upload data from ANY wearable (clients)
  1. Export data from your device (Apple Health app, Fitbit/Garmin app or website, etc.).
  2. Get the file (zip, xml, csv, json) to a computer.
  3. Use the Desktop Tool or Web App below for smart local processing + upload.

The uploader app automatically focuses on recent data and works with Apple, Fitbit, Garmin, and most other wearables. For blood tests: Use the built-in lab portal fetcher (GoodLabs, Quest, etc.) — just enter your lab login and it downloads the results for you.

📱 Open Web App (install on iPhone) 💻 Download Desktop Tool — includes automatic lab login (GoodLabs, Quest, etc.)

Want a real iOS App on the App Store using HealthKit directly? Full starter code + instructions are ready in the project (ios-app/README.md).

Other devices (Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, etc.)
  • Log into the device's web or app dashboard.
  • Look for "Export data", "Download CSV", "Health data export", or "Share data".
  • Upload the CSV, JSON, or XML file(s) here.
