Hair & Saliva Sample Collection Instructions

Follow these steps carefully for accurate results.

Step 1: Gather Materials

• Clean scissors
• 1 teaspoon of hair (about the width of a pencil)
• 2 cotton swabs (Qtips)
• Plastic zip-lock bag

Step 2: Collect Hair Sample

Cut hair from the back of the head, close to the scalp. You need approximately 1 teaspoon (full) of hair.

Step 3: Collect Saliva Sample

Rub 2 Q-tips inside your mouth (under tongue and cheeks) for 30 seconds each.

Step 4: Package & Mail

Place hair + used Q-tips in a zip-lock bag. Write your **Full Name + Birthday** on the bag.
