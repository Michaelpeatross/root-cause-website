Hair & Saliva Collection

Follow these steps carefully for accurate bioenergetic results.

Step 1 — Gather Materials

Clean scissors · ~1 teaspoon of hair · 2 cotton swabs · plastic zip-lock bag

Step 2 — Collect Hair

Cut hair from the back of the head, close to the scalp. You need approximately one full teaspoon.

Step 3 — Collect Saliva

Rub 2 Q-tips inside your mouth (under tongue and cheeks) for 30 seconds each.

Step 4 — Package & Mail

Place hair and used Q-tips in the zip-lock bag. Write your full name and birthday on the bag.
